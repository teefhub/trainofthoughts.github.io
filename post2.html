Dear Mum and Dad

I thank you for bringing me to this world and left me all alone

Posted by teef on May 25, 2023

My Mum passed away when I was an infant, so I do not have any memory of her sadly. I grew up without a mother figure and having a step mother who constantly berated and beat me up daily, it took a toll on me. I am a grown woman who does not know how to communicate with other women. I am a grown woman who does not know how to accept compliments. No one was there to teach me about gender norms, societal expectations, and nurturing behaviors.

Dad, I missed you. Sometimes I lay at night crying because I released I cannot see or talk to you anymore. You were not the best father in the world, but you tried. I know you tried. You didn't abuse me or anything when you were sober, even when you were drunk, you didn't lay a hand on me. However, you did yelled at me every time you were drunk, but that's ok to me.

I think you were still chasing you dream when you had me and my brother, you didn't know what to do in life. As an adult, I understand it. You wanted money, women, fame and no responsibilities. Everyone does. But I am grateful that you still tried, that you got a job and supported your family, that you raised me.

I missed you Dad

However, I do thank my mum for the experience of growing up without a mother as it cultivated the resilience, independence, and self-reliance in me. I moved out at 17, no help from anyone, started saving on my own, even went by days without food.

I want you to know that despite the pain of your absence, I am finding my way in this world. I have been surrounded by a loving support network, friends who have become like family, and mentors who have stepped in to offer guidance when I needed it most. They have reminded me of the values you instilled in me, and I am forever grateful for their presence in my life.

So thank you.
